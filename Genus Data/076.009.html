6. Habit and Habitat: Erect or climbing palm; mostly found in primary tropical rain forest on a great variety of soil with high- light intensities such as riverbanks

 

7. Economic Importance: The apices of several species are sought after for food. In the past, red resin from the fruits of species related to and including Daemonorops draco and D. rubra was collected as ‘dragon’s blood’ for medicinal or dyeing purposes. At one time, ‘dragon’s blood’ was an item of trade between Borneo, Sumatra and Malay Peninsula, and China.
